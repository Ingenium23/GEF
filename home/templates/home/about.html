Our Methodology

The methodology applied in the Greening Earth projects has its origin in 1985 in a rural development project. It was designed to improve living conditions in rural communities suffering from severe degradation of their natural resources that led to extreme levels of poverty. The goal of the projects using this methodology is to eliminate poverty, improve living conditions, and restore the natural environment and to introduce sustainable use of natural resources. The projects aim primarily at a change of mindset of the participating communities to bring about behavioral changes that result in sustainable development.

The core elements of the Methodology are

  •  Peer learning: Expert farmers training farmers

  •  Strong motivational elements in the form of contests between households and communities.

  •  Relatively short duration of projects (3 years)
