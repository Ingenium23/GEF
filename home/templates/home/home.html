    Aims to help the community living in the poorest areas of the world where restoration of the severely degraded landscapes play a central role and improve their living standard so that their physical, emotional and social needs are satisfied. Greening Earth foundation is the ambassador for the poorest people in the world and we insure their integral human development by being a network for organizations, companies, governments and individuals that gives their opportunities to support poorest people in an effective ways. Communities living in the poorest areas, they deserve to live in a good environment, sheltered, healthier with happiness and joy together with food security. Let’s do our part they will do their part to cherish happiness to their lives.
